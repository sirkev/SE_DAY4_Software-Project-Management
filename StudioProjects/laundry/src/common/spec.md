# Cleaning Services SaaS Platform Specification

Build a comprehensive SaaS application that connects customers with cleaning service providers for various home and business services including house cleaning, laundry, fumigation, moving services, and other maintenance tasks.

## Core User Types

**Service Providers (Cleaning Businesses)**
- Register and create business profiles with service offerings
- Manage service pricing, availability, and service areas
- Accept or decline service requests from customers
- Track earnings, completed jobs, and customer ratings
- Upload certifications, insurance documents, and portfolio photos

**Customers**
- Browse and search for available cleaning services by type and location
- View service provider profiles, ratings, and pricing
- Book services with preferred date/time scheduling
- Make secure payments and track service history
- Rate and review completed services

## Service Categories

**Primary Services:**
- House Cleaning (regular, deep cleaning, move-in/move-out)
- Laundry Services (wash & fold, dry cleaning pickup/delivery)
- Fumigation & Pest Control
- Moving Services (packing, cleaning before/after moves)
- Office/Commercial Cleaning
- Specialized Services (carpet cleaning, window cleaning, etc.)

## Key Features

**For Customers:**
- Service discovery with location-based search and filtering
- Real-time availability checking and instant booking
- Secure payment processing with multiple payment methods
- Service tracking with notifications and updates
- Review system with photo uploads of completed work
- Recurring service scheduling for regular cleaning

**For Service Providers:**
- Dashboard for managing bookings, calendar, and customer communications
- Mobile-optimized interface for field workers to update job status
- Automated invoicing and payment collection
- Customer relationship management tools
- Performance analytics and business insights
- Integration with existing business tools and accounting software

**Platform Administration:**
- User verification system for service providers (background checks, insurance validation)
- Dispute resolution system between customers and providers
- Quality assurance monitoring and provider rating system
- Geographic expansion tools for new market management
- Revenue tracking and commission management

## Technical Requirements

**User Experience:**
- Responsive web application optimized for mobile and desktop
- Progressive web app capabilities for offline booking management
- Real-time messaging between customers and service providers
- Push notifications for booking confirmations, reminders, and updates
- Multi-language support for diverse markets

**Integration Capabilities:**
- Payment gateway integration (Stripe,Mpesa,Airtel Money,PayPal, etc.)
- Calendar synchronization (Google Calendar, Outlook)
- Geolocation services for service area mapping
- SMS and email notification systems
- Background check and verification service APIs

## Business Model Features

**Revenue Streams:**
- Commission-based pricing on completed services
- Premium subscription tiers for service providers
- Featured listing and advertising options
- Processing fees for payment transactions

**Quality Assurance:**
- Service provider vetting and onboarding process
- Customer feedback aggregation and provider scoring
- Insurance and bonding verification system
- Dispute resolution and refund processing workflows
